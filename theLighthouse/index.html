Beacon lore

The Lighthouse predates the harbor by a century. Its Fresnel array is etched with five constellations, each humming at a different harmonic. The initials of these constellations form a word Moreau cherished.

Keep this in mind when you reach the lantern room. Salt preserves secrets.

Lore fragments

Harbor ordinance

The city archives mention a contingency named Project Undertow, meant to lure something sleeping beneath the reef. Funding stopped the same week Moreau took watch.

Echo Bloom memo

AI assistant logs show the phrase “When the beam fails, listen below” repeated 87 times. Bloom insists the phrase was seeded by the keeper.

Relic manifest

Among the recovered crates was a brass astrolabe engraved with the letters B.R.I.N.E. Each letter marks a notch along the rim.
